about me

I am a PhD Candidate in Economics at Central European University. My research focus on causal questions in the field of labor economics. I am interested in public challanges such as population ageing, and, more recently, in how we can use the advances in data-related fields (e.g. machine learning) for social good. For more detail, please look at my research page.

Currently, I am on leave from my studies and work as a data scientist for Emarsys. I teach various data related courses at Central European University, Budapest University of Technology and Economics, and other institutions for advanced studies such as Mathias Corvinus Collegium, and Rajk László College.

For more detail, please look at my resumé.
